MEET THE FOUNDER

Gitanjali Kumari

FOUNDER & CREATIVE DIRECTOR

Born with a deep-rooted passion for traditional craftsmanship and a keen eye for contemporary design, Gitanjali Kumari is the visionary force behind Gitanjali Couture. Her journey began with a simple desire to honor and elevate heritage textiles through modern silhouettes.

Gitanjali believes that fashion should be a harmonious blend of past and present, a seamless fusion of traditional artistry and modern sophistication.

Each collection at Gitanjali Couture is a carefully curated expression of this philosophy, meticulously crafted to tell a story of cultural richness and personal artistic expression. Her unwavering dedication to quality, detail, and empowering women through beautiful design defines the very essence of the brand.

"Gitanjali Couture is a reflection of my personal journey and artistic vision. It's about celebrating timeless beauty while embracing innovation." - Gitanjali Kumari
